FAQs

  1. What is DMC and what is it for?
    I am reviving the old DMC; the agenda is quite similar to the old one, being focused on high school math. There will be occasional forays into higher math. It will be a mixture of lectures and interactives. The inaugural revival meeting will have free boba and a plank countdown.
  2. Where and when are the meetings happening?
    They are currently slotted for Thursdays at 6PM at UCD Library, but this is subject to change after input from students and parents.
  3. What is happening at the first meeting and is it informational?
    I plan on making the first meeting both informational and also running a plank countdown for the students. (These do not conflict because I expect the parents to be asking the vast majority of the questions.)
  4. How do I sign up?
    Signup Google form is here.
  6. Who are you?
    I am a local college student and MOP alumnus ('23) who still spends quite a lot of time on contest math. Accomplishments with my name can be found on the internet. I also taught as a TA at MOP 2025 and G2 2024 and teacher at AlphaStar in 2025.
