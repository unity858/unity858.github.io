FAQs

  1. What is DMC and what is it for?
    I am reviving the old DMC; the agenda is quite similar to the old one, being focused on high school math. There will be occasional forays into higher math. It will be a mixture of lectures and interactives. The inaugural revival meeting will have free boba and a plank countdown.

  3. Who are you?
    I am a local college student and MOP alumnus ('23) who still spends quite a lot of time on contest math.
